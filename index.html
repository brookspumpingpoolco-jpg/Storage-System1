What kind of support do Meridian customers have access to?

We recognize that most of your self-storage business is managed in your facility management software and we treat it with the appropriate level of urgency.

  • Our teams are cross-trained to support all of our products
  • Our tech support team, on average, picks up a call within 60 seconds
  • Best of all? Meridian Support is entirely covered within your monthly subscription
How do I know if Meridian is a good fit for my business?

Meridian feature-rich software was built from the ground up to provide a flexible, comprehensive solution to address your most critical business challenges.

  • If you need highly-configurable daily workflows or a tailored set of vendor products to meet the needs of your business, Meridian is the solution for you
  • Meridian has something for both small and large operations
  • Our smaller operators typically tell us they appreciate the ability to choose from integrations that they use to tailor workflows specifically to their business
If I switch to Meridian, can I transfer my existing data and business information?

Short answer: yes!

We have a dedicated team committed to making it as easy as possible for you to bring your current business information from your old property management software to Meridian. The experience looks a little different depending on which software you're using today, but we'd love to have a chat with you about what that process looks like.

Does Meridian require a contract?

Meridian is billed monthly and only requires a monthly commitment. We're confident you won't want to switch after trying our solution, however, you're free to cancel at any time. After all, there's a reason that thousands of facilities trust Meridian software to help run their business.

About Meridian

Meridian was founded by a small-town, one-person team of software engineers who recognized a greater purpose for storage facility management. We saw the challenges that storage operators face every day and set out to create a solution that truly serves the entire storage community.

This software has been designed with you in mind—every feature, every workflow, and every integration was built to solve real problems faced by real storage facility owners and managers.

We understand that running a storage facility isn't just about managing units—it's about building relationships with customers, optimizing operations, and growing your business. That's why Meridian goes beyond basic management software to provide a comprehensive platform that empowers you to succeed.

From small single-facility operations to multi-location portfolios, Meridian scales with your business. Our commitment is simple: provide powerful, intuitive software that makes your life easier and your business more profitable.
